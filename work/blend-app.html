Blend

A new relationship app — built from a single word into a brand, a product, and a shipping app couples open every day.

Blend
Services
Brand Identity, App Design, Development
Year

Engagement
End-to-end build
OVERVIEWThe brief

Take Blend from a name to a finished product — logo, identity, and an app that couples actually want to open every day.

01The challenge

Most relationship apps land in one of two places: clinical and cold, or twee and overdesigned. Neither lasts more than a week on a home screen. Blend needed something in between — warm, grown-up, quietly confident — and it had to do that with no existing brand, no app, and a pre-launch team.

The work spanned every layer: name a visual world, design a daily ritual, and ship a real, working product. One team, one trajectory — so nothing got lost in handoffs.

02What we did
  • Built the full brand identity — wordmark, palette, typography, illustration, voice — from a single brief.
  • Designed the end-to-end app: onboarding, daily prompts, shared streaks and a quiet home surface that earns the open.
  • Developed and shipped the production build — clean, performant, iOS and Android ready.
  • Prototyped the core daily loop with real couples before a single line of production code.
  • Handed off a tight design system the in-house team can keep extending without losing the through-line.
03The outcome

Blend launched with an identity that earns trust on first glance and a product that earns the second open. Because brand, design and engineering came from the same room, the work moved fast and stayed coherent — a tiny team punching well above its weight from day one.
