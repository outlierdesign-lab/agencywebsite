OVERVIEWThe brief

Take Blend from a name to a live, shipping app — identity, product and code, all delivered inside the founder's launch window.

01The challenge

Pre-launch. No brand, no app, no roadmap to a working product — just a name and a tight window. And a category that punishes anything clinical or overcute.

Founders don't have time for a brand book waiting on a designer waiting on a developer. So we owned every layer — naming the visual world, designing the daily loop, writing the production code. One team, one trajectory, no handoff delays.

02What we did
  • Brand identity from zero — wordmark, palette, type, illustration, voice — delivered in rounds the team could actually review between investor calls.
  • End-to-end app design: onboarding, daily prompts, shared streaks, and a home surface that earns the second open.
  • Production build, shipped — clean, performant, iOS and Android ready.
  • Validated the core daily loop with real couples before a line of production code.
  • Handed back a tight design system the team can keep extending without losing the through-line.
03The outcome

Blend went from name to live app inside the founder's launch window. Because brand, design and engineering came from one room, there were no handoff delays — the work moved fast and stayed coherent. The founding team's focus stayed on go-to-market, not on the agency.
