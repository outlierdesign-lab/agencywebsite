OVERVIEWThe brief

Tame a vast, multi-product insurance marketplace into something a first-time buyer can navigate in minutes.

01The challenge

InsuranceDekho spans motor, health, life and more — thousands of plans from dozens of insurers, sold to a massive, mostly first-time audience across every kind of device and connection. At that scale, every extra tap and every line of jargon costs conversions.

The work had to balance three things at once: the breadth of an enterprise catalogue, the clarity a nervous buyer needs, and the consistency a fast-moving in-house team can actually maintain.

02What we did
  • Restructured the core buying journey — quote, compare and purchase — around a calm, step-by-step hierarchy.
  • Designed responsive web and app surfaces that stay legible and fast on low-end devices and patchy networks.
  • Built a scalable design system and component library so a large team could ship across many product lines consistently.
  • Cut jargon everywhere — plain-language labels, honest pricing and obvious next steps at each decision point.
03The outcome

The redesigned experience turns a famously overwhelming category into a guided, trustworthy path from question to purchase — at the scale of millions. The work supported InsuranceDekho's $70M Series C and gave the team a durable system to keep growing on.
