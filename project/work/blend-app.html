01 — Brand & App Design

Blend

A relationship-enhancing app — and the brand to make it feel warm, safe and worth opening every day.

Client
Blend
Services
Brand, App Design, UI/UX
Year
2023
Timeline
6 weeks
OVERVIEWThe brief

Help couples grow closer through small, intentional daily moments — without another noisy, gamified app.

01The challenge

Relationship apps tend to feel either clinical or cloyingly cute. Blend needed a voice somewhere in between: tender but grown-up, playful but never juvenile. The product also had to lower the barrier to a daily ritual — opening the app should feel like a small gift, not a chore.

On top of that, the team was pre-launch with no existing identity. We were starting from a name and a feeling.

02What we did
  • Built a complete brand identity — wordmark, palette, typography and a soft, tactile illustration language.
  • Designed the end-to-end product: onboarding, daily prompts, shared streaks and a calm home surface.
  • Created a reusable design system so the small team could ship new features without losing consistency.
  • Prototyped the core daily loop and tested it with real couples before a line of production code.
03The outcome

The finished product feels unmistakably Blend — warm, quiet and human. The daily ritual became the heart of the experience, and the brand gave a tiny team an outsized sense of polish that helped them stand out in early conversations.
