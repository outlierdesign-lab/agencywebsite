OVERVIEWThe brief

Make insurance feel simple and trustworthy for people buying their first policy — often on a budget phone, often unsure.

01The challenge

Insurance is dense, jargon-heavy and easy to distrust. Verak serves a huge, first-time audience where clarity isn't a nicety — it's the difference between a completed purchase and a confused drop-off.

Everything had to work fast and legibly on low-end devices and patchy connections, without ever feeling cheap.

02What we did
  • Designed an app that explains coverage in plain language, with friendly defaults and zero jargon.
  • Rebuilt the website to convert — clear value, honest pricing and an obvious next step.
  • Optimised every flow for low-end devices: light, fast and legible at any size.
  • Established a trustworthy, approachable visual identity across product and marketing.
03The outcome

Verak shipped an experience that turns a famously confusing purchase into something calm and clear. The work supported a $1.8M seed and helped the team scale trust with a fast-growing audience.
