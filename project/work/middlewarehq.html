Middleware

An engineering-analytics platform — designed to win the trust of both developers and investors.

Client
Middleware
Services
Product Design, Website
Stage
$1M Pre-Seed
Year

OVERVIEWThe brief

Turn dense engineering data into a product developers actually want to open — and a story investors believe in.

01The challenge

Developer-productivity tools live or die on credibility. The interface had to feel native to engineers — fast, dense and honest — while the marketing site had to make the value obvious to non-technical buyers and investors in seconds.

The team was raising. Every surface we touched had to do double duty: serve real users and support the round.

02What we did
  • Redesigned the core product around clarity: legible metrics, sensible defaults and a calm information hierarchy.
  • Built a conversion-focused marketing website that explains a technical product in plain language.
  • Defined a scalable design system and component library for the in-house team.
  • Partnered closely through the raise, iterating on narrative and visuals as the story sharpened.
03The outcome

Middleware shipped a product that feels credible to its toughest audience — engineers — and a site that makes the value land immediately. The work supported a $1M pre-seed and gave the team a design foundation to keep building on.

"Outstanding design services! The team's creativity and attention to detail exceeded our expectations. Helped us scale the product and sales."
Jayant Bhawal
Co-founder, Middleware
