Brand Identity Designer

Full-time

What you'll do

  • Own brand identity engagements end-to-end — research, exploration, refinement, documentation.

Who you are

Internship · 3–6 months

What you'll do

  • Design product features, marketing pages, social systems and visual assets across live client engagements.
